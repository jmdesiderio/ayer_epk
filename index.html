About

AYER is a New York City based project led by vocalist and electronic recording artist Danny Schmittler. The result of collaborations with an international roster of producers, AYER's recorded music and live full-band shows infuse nostalgic disco, r&b and electro-pop.

Releases

AYER originals and remixes are favorites on Hype Machine and 8tracks; since the first release in October 2013, AYER's music has reached listen counts in the millions on both Spotify and SoundCloud.

With a focus on collaboration, AYER is amassing an impressive portfolio of visual arts. His latest collaboration with Rick Day and Steve Benisty, a music video for the first single off the In My Headphones EP, was featured in Huffington Post.

Live

Represented by The Agency Group and led by Jason Desiderio, AYER's full band set-up includes guitar, keys, bass, drums and back-up vocals. Custom projections and venue specific installations make for uniquely memorable experiences. AYER can also perform as a two, three and four-piece band.

AYER has shared line-ups with ASTR, Ghost Beach, MOTHXR, VÉRITÉ, Teen Daze and others at NYC and LA's top indie venues.

Residency @ PIANOS in NYC AYER is curating a Tuesday night residency at Pianos, the first four Tuesdays in June. On at 9PM all four nights, AYER will be cushioned between some of NYC's best up and coming indie bands and performers.
